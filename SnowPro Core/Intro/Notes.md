## COF-C03

### SNOWPRO CORE CERTIFICATION OVERVIEW

• Use the Snowflake AI Data Cloud architecture

• Manage Snowflake accounts and virtual warehouses

• Perform data loading, unloading, and transformation

• Use structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data

• Monitor and optimize performance

• Enable data collaboration and protection

• Establish Snowflake connectivity

### EXAM FORMAT

**Exam Version**: COF-C03 <br>
**Total Number of Questions**: 100 <br>
**Question Types**: Multiple Select, Multiple Choice <br>
**Time Limit**: 115 minutes <br>
**Languages**: English, Japanese, Korean, French, Spanish <br>
**Registration fee**: $175 USD <br>
**India Registration fee**: $140 USD <br>
**Passing Score**: 750 + Scaled Scoring from 0 - 1000 <br>
**Unscored Content**: Exams may include unscored items to gather statistical information for future use. These items are not identified on the form and do not impact your score, and additional time is factored into account for this content. <br>
**Prerequisites**: No prerequisites <br>

Delivery Options:

1. Online Proctoring
2. Onsite Testing Centers

## Practice Exams
There's also the option to register for practice exams; which have the following policies:
 * Once purchased, candidates will have 24 hours to access and complete their practice exams.
 *  The practice exam can only be taken once and cannot be retaken once submitted for scoring.
 * If a candidate does not access the practice exam within the 24-hour window, they will forfeit their registration fee and cannot re-register until 48 hours after the original purchase.
